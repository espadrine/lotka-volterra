Lotka Volterra
Algorithmic Simulation

Synopsis

Predators eat preys. But the scarcer the prey, the hungrier the predator. Empty stomach kills. But if predators die, there is nobody left to hunt down preys, which lets them procreate happily! Which makes them easier to hunt. More information at Lotka-Volterra.

  • Predators eat adjacent preys and procreate after their meal. If they haven't had a meal, they die.
  • Preys get pregnant if adjacent to bare ground.
  • If adjacent to bare ground, predators and preys will move.

Parameters

Time taken by each step: 10
Initial percentage of predators: 0.02
Initial percentage of prey: 0.2
Probability of death of predators: 0.02
Probability of birth of predators: 0.9
Probability of birth of prey: 0.04
