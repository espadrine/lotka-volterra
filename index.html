Lotka Volterra
Algorithmic Simulation

Synopsis

Men eat deer. But the less deer there are, the less men can eat. Empty stomach kills. But if men die, there is nobody left to hunt down deer, which lets them procreate happily! Which makes them easier to hunt. More information at Lotka-Volterra.

  • Men eat adjacent deer and procreate after their meal. If they haven't had a meal, they die.
  • Deer get pregnant if adjacent to bare ground.
  • If adjacent to bare ground, men and deer will move.

Parameters

Time taken by each step: 10
Initial percentage of men: 0.02
Initial percentage of deer: 0.2
Probability of death of men: 0.02
Probability of birth of men: 0.9
Probability of birth of deer: 0.04
